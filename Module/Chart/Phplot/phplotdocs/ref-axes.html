6.9. Axis Controls

The functions in this section control aspects of the X and Y axes. You can control the axis position and optionally use logarithmic scaling. You can also prevent drawing the axis lines.

  • SetDrawXAxis

  • SetDrawYAxis

  • SetXAxisPosition

  • SetXScaleType

  • SetYAxisPosition

  • SetYScaleType
